About us

Ever since we began trading back in 2002, we’ve focused on providing the very best foreign exchange services to our clients.

The History of Global Reach Partners

From humble beginnings, we’ve grown to become a leading provider in the world of foreign exchange.

Our experience has taught us a lot and we’ve been able to build an outstanding team. Over the years, we’ve evolved to become a 170strong organisation that assists corporate clients in ﬁnding the most effective approach to international payments, treasury and risk management.

In August 2016, we acquired FC Exchange, who create bespoke currency solutions for individuals to assist with their private foreign exchange requirements and who are now fully integrated into the wider Global Reach Group. We pride ourselves on our levels of service and take time to understand each of our clients’ unique currency needs so that we can help them put the right strategy in place.

It reﬂects our intelligent foreign exchange ethos, which itself is a product of our years of experience and expertise.

An Established Provider

We are a trusted and well-established business that securely handles billions of Pounds worth of currency each year, across a wide range of over 120 different denominations.

We have an equally diverse client database, including FTSE100 listed companies

As a registered Money Services Business (MSB) we’re regulated by HM Revenue and Customs, and subject to money laundering regulations. Plus, to ensure complete security we operate client transaction accounts.

We’re supervised by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), under the Payment Services Directive (2009), which ensures that we act in a way that puts our clients ﬁrst, while our subsidiary, Global Reach Markets, is fully authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority.
